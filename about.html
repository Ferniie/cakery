About Munamii

At Munamii Cakery, each cake, each box of cupcakes that we make has a very special value for us.
We not only want to bring a dessert to your table but rather a unique experience for you, which will become an unforgettable memory.
We have been in the pastry market for more than 8 years and now we offer you a new way to shop through our website.

Munamii is a small business owned by Munamii, who is a passionate baker and cake designer.
She has a great love for baking and creating beautiful cakes and cupcakes for all occasions.
Munamii has a background in pastry arts and has honed her skills over the years to create delicious and visually stunning desserts.

Munamii is dedicated to using high-quality ingredients and creating custom designs that reflect the unique style and preferences of her clients.
She takes pride in her work and strives to provide exceptional customer service to ensure that each client has a positive experience.
Munamii is always looking for new ways to innovate and improve her craft, and she is excited to share her passion for baking with the world.

We are located in Los Angeles, but we deliver to other countries.
We are happy to help you with your order and we are looking forward to hearing from you!

If you have any questions or inquiries, please feel free to reach out to Munamii through the contact page.
